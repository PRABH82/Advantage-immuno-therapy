How Cancer Cells Evade the Immune System

Cancer cells use multiple tricks to grow in our body. Advantage Immunotherapy uses a master protocol to stop them.

1 Block Their Maturation

Cancer cells prevent immune cells from developing into mature, effective fighters capable of recognizing and attacking tumors.

2 Block Their Interaction With Other Immune System Cells

Tumors disrupt communication between immune cells, preventing coordinated attacks and weakening the overall immune response.

3 Suppress Their Normal Biological Activity

Cancer cells release signals that dampen immune cell function, essentially putting the immune system to sleep while the tumor grows unchecked.

4 Steal Energy From These Cells And Other Immune Cells

Tumors create a microenvironment that deprives immune cells of nutrients and energy, leaving them too weak to mount an effective defense.

Baseline eligibility requirements

Solid Tumors Only

We treat patients with solid tumors that can be biopsied by our medical staff. Blood malignancies such as leukemia cannot be treated at this time.

Stable Immune System

Any immunotherapy requires a stable, functioning immune system. Patients with immune deficiencies may not qualify until normal activity is restored.

Ability to Travel

Treatment is administered exclusively at our facility in Las Vegas, Nevada. Patients must be medically cleared for travel.

Six-Week Treatment Cycle

Three separate treatments, each two weeks apart. Stay six weeks in Las Vegas or visit twice for two weeks at a time.

Medical Records Review

Pathology reports, imaging, and recent labs are reviewed by our team during the evaluation process.

Selective Acceptance

Frequently Asked Questions

What conditions do you treat?

We treat patients with solid tumors that can be biopsied by our medical staff. Blood malignancies such as leukemia are not currently treatable with our protocol.

Where is the treatment administered?

Treatment is administered exclusively at our facility in Las Vegas, Nevada, by a fully credentialed medical team.

How long is the treatment cycle?

Our protocol consists of three separate treatments, two weeks apart. Patients can stay in Las Vegas for the full six weeks or travel twice for two weeks at a time.

Is this an experimental drug?

No. Our treatment uses your own dendritic cells, loaded with mRNA and proteins specific to your cancer. No foreign compounds or genetically modified cells are introduced into your body.

How do I apply for treatment?

Begin with a free intro call. If you meet baseline eligibility, our team will schedule a medical evaluation to determine if our treatment is right for you.
